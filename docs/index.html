Half Iron-Man Challenge

The goal is to find out if the average person can wake up and complete a half-ironman with no training at all, using me as the test subject

This challenge will take place completely inside the USF REC on Wednesday, April 22nd

How it works:

  • I have one day, April 22nd, to complete a half ironman marathon. This consists of a 1.2 mile swim, a 56 mile bike, and a 13.1 mile run
  • To raise money for charity, I have opened bids/donations that go to charity
  • If I complete the challenge, you pay for your own bid
  • If I fail, I will match your donation and it will cost you nothing

All donations go 100% to UNICEF, a charity focused on providing humanitarian aid to children around the world
